Bio.  I am a Physics Engineer from the University of Liège who is passionate about exploring the intersection of physics and computer science. Since 2023, I have been pursuing my PhD under the supervision of professors Marilaure Grégoire and Gilles Louppe. My thesis, entitled Understanding ocean deoxygenation using deep learning, is being conducted within the framework of the Multiple Threats on Ocean Health project, funded by the European Space Agency.
