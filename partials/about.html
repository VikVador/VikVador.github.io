I am a PhD student at the University of Liège working at the intersection of oceanography, modeling, and deep learning. My research focuses on understanding ocean deoxygenation using deep learning, under the co-supervision of Marilaure Grégoire and Gilles Louppe (MAST and SAIL teams). I develop generative neural emulators of large-scale ocean dynamics to explore scientific questions and interpret results through the lens of physics and biogeochemistry.
