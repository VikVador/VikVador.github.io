Almanac

iOS App • In Development

SwiftUI

A minimalist iOS study companion that teaches students how to break down coursework into realistic, achievable goals. Unlike existing productivity apps that overwhelm with features, Almanac guides users through a structured process to create clear study plans in under 5 minutes, and then gets out of your way.
